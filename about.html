ABOUT IZZY BURGER

Izzy Burger is a Cape Town-based fast-food brand. It was established in 2016 and started off as a small takeaway outlet. Izzy Burger specializes in flame-grilled gourmet style burgers with fresh ingredients. The fast-food brand has grown into popularity due to its unique menu and social media presence. Izzy Burger now operates 3 main stores in Cape Town
